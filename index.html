Procedure

Life Of PY will be an event comprising of a team of 2 people, and the resigstrations will be open for all category with no prequistes from the participants. It will contain a total of 3-5 proble statements from different genre of Python which will be available to all the participants from start of the event. Anyone can start solving in any order and submit multiple solutions.

Scoring Pattern

Each problem statement will have its own requirement for scoring and submisiions will be judged on the same basis. The most optimized submission will be given highest marks followed by subsequent optimized submissions. The final scoreboard will be prepared from collective scores in problems solved by the team. From the final scoreboard, top 10 teams will be eligible for he prizes.

Benifit to Qualified Teams

The top 10 teams eligible for prizes will be awarded with goodies from Neodristi partner (i.e. Devfolio) and will get direct entry to finale main event i.e. "Tame the Python" in OJASS'20

What is “Tame the Python”?

Tame the Python is one of our 5 main events under NeoDrishti which focuses on Python development tools and libraries. The event structure is as follows:

Prelims: Prelims will be conducted on spot during OJASS’20 this year in the month of February.

(Expected Dates between 28th Feb and 1st March). This round will contain MCQ problems based on basics of python and 10 teams from this prelims will be selected for Finale.

Finale: Finale round will combine participants from the on-spot prelims and the Hackathon as mentioned earlier and the teams selected will be participating in the Finale which is divided into two sub-sections, namely

1) Basic CLI Python projects on libraries which will be published on our official Facebook page before the event (At least 3 weeks before).

2) Teams will then participate in a competitive coding based event named as Code Golf which aims to minimize the number of letters used to solve problem using python.

Final scores from both sub-sections will be calculated and winners will be announced on the spot.
